Welcome to Regatta Group careers site

Welcome to the Regatta Group Careers Site – your one stop site to access the very latest exciting job opportunities available with our amazing brands across head office in Manchester, Retail across the UK and logistics in Ellesmere Port. We’re proud of our brands, Regatta, Craghoppers and Dare2b and even prouder of our people, all of whom relish finding customer focused and innovative opportunities to grow our business.


Here you can find out more about what it is like to work at the Group and the excellent benefits you receive as part of your remuneration package. We recruit people with an entrepreneurial spirit, a can do attitude, the ability to build great relationships and, of course, a great work ethic. We look for people with a passion for what they do who can demonstrate great judgement, innovation and impact.


We are family owned and whilst we have grown to over 1100 employees globally, we still have a family feel.
