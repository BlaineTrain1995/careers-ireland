About Craghoppers

Purpose

To inspire people to fulfil their dreams of travelling and exploring the world.


Vision

To be acknowledged as the global brand authority, for world travel and outdoor adventure clothing.


Mission

To offer our customers the best outdoor and travel clothing that is always technical, contemporary and fit for purpose.

Our brand

Back in 1965, Craghoppers started like most epic adventures do - a couple of competitive outdoor enthusiasts, a few new ideas and a bit of ‘I bet you can’t’. Our founders challenged themselves to design the ultimate clothing for the harshest adventure - and then to test it by wearing it from the beautiful British Peak District to the heights of Everest. We’ve since made it our life’s work. And we’ve been travelling the world for inspiration and designing travel and outdoor gear to cope with everything we’ve encountered. It’s a winning formula that’s made us Britain’s leading authority in travel, outdoor and adventure clothing.


You know you've been part of a growing success when you can look back and reflect on achievements, adventures and fabulous memories. And the best bit? It all makes you want to do even more and more... It's our passion and our experience of travel that inspires us to experiment further and further with our essential travel clothing. It's a never ending cycle because the minute we see our fresh designs in fabric, we just want to get them out there and prove what they can do. This momentum is the essence of our brand and it's what keeps us keen, keeps you ready for anything and brings the rest of the world closer to your comfort zone.
